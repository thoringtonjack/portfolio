About the Studio

Design is a
language.
I speak it fluently.

Studio Noir is an independent graphic design practice based in New York, specializing in brand identity, editorial systems, and art direction. Founded in 2017, the studio has worked with cultural institutions, luxury brands, and ambitious startups across three continents.

Every project begins with the same question: what does this want to feel like? The answer shapes everything — from the weight of a typeface to the silence between elements.
